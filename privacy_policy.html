ManabiDojo - Learn Japanese with Crunchyroll and Netflix - Privacy Policy

When users use our Extension, we collect the following personal data:

  • Google Authentication Data
    When the user logs in via Google OAuth, we collect the Google subject ID and email address. These are sent to our server and stored in our database to identify and authenticate your account.
  • Login Activity
    Each time the user logs into the Extension, we record the date and time of the last login for activity tracking and user experience improvement.
  • AI Feature Usage
    Each time the user logs into the Extension, we record the date and time of the last login for activity tracking and user experience improvement.
  • Flashcard Data
    The user can create flashcards within the Extension, including word meanings and custom text. These flashcards are stored and associated with user's account.
To maintain the login session, we use cookies to store a refresh token in the browser. This token allows the Extension to keep users logged in without requiring to re-authenticate each time. We only use cookies for this specific purpose and do not use cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising.

We use the collected information to:
  • Authenticate and manage user accounts
  • Improve user experience by tracking usage trends
  • Monitor and optimize AI feature access
  • Store and display user-generated content (flashcards)
  • Maintain login sessions using refresh tokens stored in cookies
We do not use user personal data for advertising or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

We do not sell, rent, or trade user personal data.

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access. This includes secure communication protocols and restricted access to our systems.

No further information about the user or their activities is collected, stored, or transmitted, other than that described above.
